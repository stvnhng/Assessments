    Introduction

    Hola! Thank you for visiting my profile! Allow me to introduce myself. The name is Steven Hong and I was born and raised in the DMV (Washington D.C., Maryland, Virginia) area. I moved to San Diego in August of 2016 after deciding it was time for a change. "Why San Diego?" many have asked. Well my best friend who was in the Navy at the time was stationed here in Coronado and invited me to visit and the rest was history. I took advantage of having a place to stay while I settle to life in San Diego and now here I am!

    Why Code?

    I guess you could say I am where I am today because for the past year, I have grown bored with my career which made me venture out to trying something new. While I was doing some research during my free time, the idea of coding had been intriguing because I find it neat that I could create a product or a website that I can envision. Coding has also caught my attention because I see a growing demand in the job industry. I also see coding as the foundation of current trends in social media and ecommerce. I also believe that it will have an impact in further advancements in future technology which I find exciting.
